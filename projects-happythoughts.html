Happy Thoughts

DATE:

January 2020

DESCRIPTION:

Happy Thoughts is an anti-cyberbullying platform to "bully-proof your thoughts!"

This provides a service that uses AI to analyze the sentiment of your “thoughts”, or messages. It aims to reduce cyberbullying by scoring messages on a happiness scale of zero to five, five being the happiest can be! Messages with negative scores are not allowed to be posted and you will recieve an error message. All messages are organized in a chart for data analysis of score frequencies.

TECHNOLOGIES:

HTML, CSS, TypeScript, Ionic 4, Angular 8, Chart.js, Firebase
