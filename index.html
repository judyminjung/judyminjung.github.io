Hi, my name is Judy Minjung Song.

I am a junior at the University of Southern California pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science and Business Administration. I am interested in the intersection between technology and the environment and how new innovations can lead to more efficient energy consumption and lessen our carbon footprint. I recognize that the business part of any product or service development is vital to the final result of its environmental impact. I aspire to intern with company with similar values where I can expand my knowledge and contribute meaningfully.

I am passionate about working for the community and fighting for climate justice and equality for marginalized communities. Outside of my academic career, I stay involved as a social media manager at Bloom Boutique which is a sustainable vintage boutique that counteracts fast fashion and donates 100% of profits to various organizations and charities.
